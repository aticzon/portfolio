About Me

I'm originally from the Bay Area and, now, I am a student at Cornell University. I study Information Science in the college of Arts and Sciences with concentrations in UX Design and Interactive Technologies.

I believe that novel ideas can bring about the next big thing to impact people lives. I even believe that through tweaking a few things in an existing design can make individuals want to use the item in their everyday life. Just like how the computer went from large bulky machines that only a few people knew how to use to becoming light weight machines that almost everyone knows how to use. For it is the combination of innovative thought and thoughtful design is a combination for success.

When I'm not rambling off about the importance of thoughtful design you can find me out in nature enjoying my photography hobby with my 9lb dog by my side.

If you want to reach out or ask any questions email me at amt258@cornell.edu.
